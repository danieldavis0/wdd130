Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this rafting website is to provide information on what rafting services and equipment are available, who is able to use these services, and build up revenue.

Audience

Who are the target customers?

The target customers would be anyone ages teen to middle-aged, who has interest in outdoor adventure type activities, with enough spare money to be able to rent out the right equipment and services.

What do they want?

The customers want to have an exciting outdoor experience without compromising on safety, convenience, or price.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?

They expect to find locations and times when river rafting is available; as well as the cost and the total number of people that can go per trip.
