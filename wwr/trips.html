Book a trip!

Try our Calm Waters trip, designed for anyone wanting a more gentle ride. Reletively short, but the most scenic of our options.

Water Splash is our standard option, offering a good amount of rapids for a fair distance, without skimping on the scenery.

Raging Rapids is our most intense option, sure to please the most daring adventurer, with endless rapids and waterfalls. Very little scenery.

Looking for a longer option? Be sure to try Splash Falls for the longest trip that we offer! Moderate amount of rapids and scenery.
